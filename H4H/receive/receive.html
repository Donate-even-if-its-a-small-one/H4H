RECEIVE DONATION

1.To act as a bridge between the privileged and the needy –Philanthrophists who wish to donate can look for all details of nearby NGO’s.Similarly,NGO’s who wish to receive offerings can fill their details in our website.These registered NGO’s will be shown to the user. That’s how we bridge the gap .

2.To make many unknown NGOs or charities recognized-Many NGO’s are in high need of resources to run and grow their organisations.They are ready to accept any donations that come their way.They need a platform for their NGO to get recognized.”Receive” option in our website provides this platform.

3.Our aim is to encourage society to support the underprivileged-There are many philanthrophists in and around our surrounding who are ready to lend their help.Our website helps these givers by providing complete information about local NGO’s.It gives information about the location of NGO,donations they accept etc.
